About Anuj

I am Anuj Keal, a Delhi-based artist, writer, and student of philosophy. My work explores the unseen spaces within us—the emotional layers, silent contradictions, and timeless questions that shape our inner worlds.

From poetic reflections to abstract art, my creations are expressions of the soul’s search for meaning. My debut book, Confessed on God and Beauty, is both a personal confession and a universal inquiry into what it means to be human.

“Art is not what I make—it’s what I reveal.”
